About Me

I'm Zibuyile Sithagu, a passionate UI/UX Designer and Front-End Developer dedicated to creating exceptional digital experiences. With expertise in user-centered design principles and modern front-end technologies, I craft intuitive, accessible, and visually stunning web applications that prioritize user needs and business goals.

As a UI/UX Designer, I specialize in user research, wireframing, prototyping, and creating seamless user flows. My front-end development skills span HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and frameworks like React, ensuring pixel-perfect implementation of design systems and responsive layouts. I believe great design is invisible—it's when users can accomplish their goals effortlessly without thinking about the interface.

My approach combines strategic design thinking with technical implementation, focusing on accessibility compliance, performance optimization, and creating scalable design systems. Whether it's crafting a landing page that converts or building a complex web application, I ensure every project delivers meaningful value to users while meeting business objectives.
